Super Energy is a 2D topdown shoot em’ up which combines classical elements of the shooter genre with contemporary ideas in resource management. Super Energy is a vigorously energetic and forceful game featuring fast-paced combat. However, the crux of the gameplay lies in it’s unique approach to armoury. In Super Energy, there is no concept of bullets or ammo. Instead, the player is equipped with a stunningly powerful electric powered laser blaster—without portable batteries. The laser blaster may only be used when it is plugged into an electrical socket. The player must defend themself against hoards of enemies whilst also making sure to remain plugged into a socket for combat abilities.
