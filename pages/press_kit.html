Press Kit

1.     Marketing Goal

Super Energy's marketing goal is to produce positive consumer awareness regarding our game. Thus, our promotional material aim to invite the player to continue investigating the game. We stand by the model that strangers are attracted to become visitors; then are leads in a sale; which then become a customer; and finally, they are fans who themselves attract other strangers to the game. This cyclical marketing strategy is employed to maximize potential for lower costs and effort.

2.     Marketing Message & Tagline

Super Energy's marketing message is to "remember to plug in;" that is, we are referring to the fact that Super Energy has a unique restriction on when and where the player can fire bullets—this restriction is in the form of electricity—the player needs electricity to fire their gun. Thus, our slogan ties into the core game mechanic, constantly reminding players of this.

3.     Target Demographic

Super Energy is primarily aimed at teenagers and adults.

4.     Standout Mechanic

The crux of the gameplay in Super Energy is in the weapon system. There is no concept of ammo or bullets. Instead, the player has unlimited ammunition. THe player is equipped with a stunningly powerful electric laster blaster. However, since the blaster doesn't have portable batters—because the folks at Laser Blaster Inc. forgot to install batteries—the laser blaster may only be used when it is plugged into an electrical socket.
